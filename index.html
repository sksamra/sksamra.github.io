Introduction


Doctor Who is a British science fiction TV show produced by BBC. The show started in 1963 and stopped airing after 1996 (classical era). The show however, was rebooted in 2005 and is still being produced today (new era). The show is about an alien named the Doctor from the Timelord race that are capable of regenerating. The Doctor travels in time and space in his/her ship, the TARDIS, which is disguised as a blue policebox. The Doctor has multiple companions from Earth that help him/her in defeating evil species.

In this article we will analyze the most popular Doctor of all time and their top 5 episodes.
